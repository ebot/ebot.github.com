As part of my site redesign, I implemented "haml":http://haml.hamptoncatlin.com/ for template rendering and "sass":http://haml.hamptoncatlin.com/docs/ for stylesheet rendering.

I love sass and the reason is simple, variables.  Let's say I want to change from green to orange when the "Flyers":http://flyers.nhl.com/ are in the playoffs.  I just change the variable values and it is reflected everywhere.  Global Search and replace be gone.

<pre name="code" class="jscript:nocontrols:nogutter">
!bg_color = black
!fore_color = white
!accent_color = #FFFD7B
!offset_color = #515151
!highlight_color = #73B34D
!hover_color = #22222
 
body
  :background-color = !bg_color
  :color = !fore_color
  a:link
    :color = !highlight_color
  a:visited
    :color = !highlight_color
  a:active
    :color = !hover_color
  a:hover
    :color = !hover_color
  
// Header & navigation settings
 
#header
  :border-bottom = "solid 1px " + !offset_color
</pre>
